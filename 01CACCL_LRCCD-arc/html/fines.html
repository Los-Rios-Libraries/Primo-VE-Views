How to Pay Fines

During the temporary library and campus closure due to COVID-19, it is not possible to pay fines. No registration (PeopleSoft) holds will be placed during this time

All materials checked out from a Los Rios Library will not be due until onground college operations resume. Please do not return items until the colleges reopen.

All due dates have been updated until June 8th or 9th. These due dates may be extended again if the colleges remain closed. If the due date is extended again, you will receive another email informing you of the new due date.

If you have any questions, please contact the ARC Library

See our Library Research & Instruction During Campus Closure guide for more information on library resources and services during the Closure.
