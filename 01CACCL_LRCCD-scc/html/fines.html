How to Pay Fines

Pay fines by cash or check at the Library Circulation Desk on the 2nd floor of the Learning Resource Center. You can also pay fines by cash or check at the Davis Center or West Sacramento Center.

To pay fines by credit card, first visit the Library Circulation Desk to get a receipt. You can then pay by credit card at the SCC Business Services Office in Rodda Hall North.

Fines totaling over $4.99 will result in a hold being placed on your record. You will not be able to register for classes until you have cleared your Library fines.

Questions about your fines? Please contact the Library Circulation Desk at (916) 558-2301.
