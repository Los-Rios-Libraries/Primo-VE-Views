How to Pay Fines

Pay fines at the Library Circulation Desk at the main Folsom campus or El Dorado Center. You can pay your fines using:

  • Cash
  • Check
  • Credit/Debit Card

Credit/debit card transactions will be processed at the FLC Business Services Office in College Administration CA-122.

Questions about your fines? Please contact the Library Circulation Desk at (916) 608-6613.
