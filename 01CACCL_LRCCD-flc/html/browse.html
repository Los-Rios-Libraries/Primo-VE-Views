Browse by Call Number

Enter part or all of a Library of Congress (LC) call number to find items in the library (such as books, DVDs, etc.)

Examples:

  • TR
  • QM
  • TR6.U62 W376 2015
  • QM23.2 .B53 2010

Find the letters and titles of the main LC classes in the Library of Congress Classification Outline .

Browse by Subject

Enter an LC subject heading to find associated items held by the library.

Examples:

  • Developmental Psychology
  • Political Theory
  • Human Anatomy
  • Photography

The Library of Congress lists current subject headings in freely available PDF files .
